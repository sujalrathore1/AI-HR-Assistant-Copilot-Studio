HR Chatbot Knowledge Base
AI-Powered HR Assistant Knowledge Base for Microsoft Copilot Studio
This document contains a structured HR knowledge base designed for an AI-powered HR Assistant chatbot built
using Microsoft Copilot Studio. The chatbot helps employees with HR-related queries including leave policies,
attendance, work-from-home policies, salary information, onboarding, benefits, and technical support.
Q1. What is the leave policy?
Answer: Employees are entitled to 12 casual leaves and 10 sick leaves annually. Leaves should be applied through
the HR portal at least one day in advance.
Q2. Can employees work from home?
Answer: Employees can work from home with manager approval and must remain available during office hours.
Q3. What are the office timings?
Answer: Office working hours are Monday to Friday from 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM.
Q4. When is salary credited?
Answer: Salary is credited on the last working day of every month.
Q5. What is the interview process?
Answer: The interview process includes resume screening, technical interview, HR interview, and final selection.
Q6. What benefits are provided to employees?
Answer: Employees receive health insurance, paid leave, provident fund benefits, and learning opportunities.
Q7. How can I reset my password?
Answer: Employees can reset passwords through the IT support portal or by contacting the IT helpdesk.
Q8. What is the notice period?
Answer: The standard notice period is 60 days unless otherwise mentioned in the employment agreement.
Q9. How do I resign from the company?
Answer: Employees must submit their resignation through the HR portal and complete exit formalities.
Q10. Is overtime allowed?
Answer: Overtime requires manager approval and will be compensated according to company policy.
Q11. What is the probation period?
Answer: The probation period for new employees is 6 months.
Q12. How can interns get certificates?
Answer: Internship certificates are provided after successful completion of the internship program.
Q13. What should I do if my laptop is not working?
Answer: Employees should immediately raise a ticket with the IT support team.
Q14. How can I check company holidays?
Answer: Employees can access the holiday calendar through the HR portal.
Q15. What is the dress code?
Answer: Employees are expected to wear business casual attire during office hours.
Q16. How can I contact HR?
Answer: Employees can contact HR through the company email or internal HR support portal.
Q17. What documents are required during onboarding?
Answer: Employees must submit identification proof, educational documents, and bank details during onboarding.
Q18. Can employees apply for unpaid leave?
Answer: Employees may apply for unpaid leave with manager and HR approval.
Q19. What happens during employee onboarding?
Answer: New employees receive company orientation, policy training, and system access setup during onboarding.
Q20. How can employees raise support tickets?
Answer: Employees can raise support tickets through the internal helpdesk portal.
Q21. Are training programs available?
Answer: Yes, employees can enroll in internal learning and development programs.
Q22. What is the attendance policy?
Answer: Employees are expected to maintain regular attendance and inform managers in case of absence.
Q23. Can employees change their shift timings?
Answer: Shift timing changes require manager approval and operational feasibility.
Q24. How is employee performance evaluated?
Answer: Performance evaluations are conducted quarterly based on goals, productivity, and teamwork.
Project Usage:
This knowledge base can be integrated into Microsoft Copilot Studio to create an AI-powered HR Assistant chatbot
capable of automatically answering employee queries and improving HR support efficiency.
